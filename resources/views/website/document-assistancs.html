Document Assistance

Once we received the admission letters from the college/university, our counselors call the students or their guardians for preparation of different documents which are required by the embassy of a country to issue a student visa. Our counselors guides the students to prepare all the required documents and arrange the visa preparation classes to the students who need to face the visa interview.

We are up to date on the latest visas information, rules and changes being made as we are in regular touch with all visa offices and Embassy that has certainly ensured that we can achieve a high visa success rate.

Following are the list of documents that the student has to deliver:

Passport
Academic Certificates (Notarized by USEF-for US only- or University Exam Department or Public Notary) ILETS/TOEFL/PTE/CAE score Statement of Purpose
Students' CV
Proof of Financial Capacity
Lately taken Photographs
Provisional and Character Certificates (Certified by Notary Public), Extra Curricular Activities Certificates (Certified by Notary Public)
2-3 Letter of Recommendation from Last School or College Attended
Miscellaneous if asked by the universities.
